The RFID application is used to log a registered user's name and timestamp per scanned RFID tag. The RFID tag number will be assigned to a person when the register. This number is unique.

The Database Administrator (DBA) will provide the registered user the physical RFID tag. In addition the DBA can schedule reports to be generated automatically for a specified individual or group.
